# Monte Carlo Simulation — Results

## 1. Estimate pi
2,000,000 random points → **pi ≈ 3.1420** (error 0.0005).

## 2. Portfolio risk
Initial 10,000, μ=8%/yr, σ=20%/yr, 252 days, 20,000 simulations.

- Mean final value: **10,828**
- 5th percentile: **7,617**
- **95% Value-at-Risk: 2,383** (potential loss vs initial)
