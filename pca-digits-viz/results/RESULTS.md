# PCA on Digits — Results

Dataset: scikit-learn `load_digits` (1,797 × 64), standardized.

- PC1 + PC2 explain **21.6%** of variance (12.0% + 9.6%).
- **31 principal components** capture **90%** of the variance (from 64 original dimensions).

See `pca_scatter.png` (2D projection by digit) and `scree.png`.
